Oil Paintings

Sarah Wierschem has done several different types of oil paintings, from landscapes to still lifes to her personal favorite, animals. A few of her earlier paintings included various types of reptiles including snakes, lizards, frogs, and turtles. She also paints a landscape for every trip out of state she does, such as her painting of Machu Picchu and the Grand Canyon. Currently, she paints mostly birds and most recently has painted a barn owl, a pelican, and is working on a heron.

Drawings

Sarah likes to do a lot of drawing. Majority of finished drawings are inked and colored in with alcohol markers, but she also does a lot more black and white ink darwings. She likes to draw things from nature, fictional places, and fictional creatures. She loves to create monsters and design characters and has created hundreds of scetches of monster busts. She likes to use hatching and rougher linework when doing this.

CTK Projects

Sarah is currently majoring in Creative Technologies at Illinois State University and is taking courses in this major. She is has taken CTK 201 or Basic Skills in the Digital Arts and is currently taking CTK 301 or Web Design and Development.
